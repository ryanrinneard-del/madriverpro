Welcome to golf.

You just picked one of the best games on Earth. Here's everything you need in your first season — one step at a time.

🐻

Here's the thing nobody told me when I started playing: every single person on the course was bad at golf once. Every pro you've ever heard of topped balls, whiffed shots, and lost golf balls in their first year. That's not just something adults say to make you feel better — it's actually how golf works. You get better slowly, one swing at a time, by showing up.

So here's the deal. This page has three phases. Start with week one. When you feel ready, move to first month. When you've played a few real rounds, check in with first season. No rush.

🐿

One more thing. You're going to hit bad shots. You're going to hit them in front of your parents, your friends, and total strangers. That's the game. Every golfer who ever lived has been there.

The players who end up good — and the players who end up loving golf — are the ones who took the next swing and kept going. That's the trick. Take the next one.

What's Elite?

Elite is the top tier of the junior program. Tournament players. Serious scorers. Kids who want to play college golf or go further. You don't pick Elite — Ryan invites you when he thinks you're ready.

There's no rush. Just keep playing, keep logging rounds, and keep getting better. If Elite is in your future, it'll come.
