What is Eight Weeks to Mastery?

Not another tips book. This is a complete, week-by-week system designed to help you build real, lasting improvement — the same approach Ryan uses with his students at Mad River Golf Club.

⛳

Technical

Fundamentals, swing mechanics, and short game — built progressively so each week layers on the last.

🧠

Mental

Pre-shot routines, on-course confidence, and the mindset shifts that separate practice from performance.

📋

Strategic

Course management, decision-making, and structured practice plans so every session has a purpose.
