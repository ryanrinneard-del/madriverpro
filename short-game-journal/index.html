How to Use This Journal

One Book. Eight Weeks. Four Sessions.

This journal mirrors the Short Game Mastery book chapter for chapter. Each week has four session types — before you practice, during your session, after you're done, and a structured drill tracker to keep you honest.

You don't need to fill out everything every time. The goal is honest, consistent attention to what's actually happening in your short game — not more pressure. Even one note per session makes a difference. Your entries save automatically in this browser.
