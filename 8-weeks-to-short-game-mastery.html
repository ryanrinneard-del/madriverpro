What is Short Game Mastery?

The companion program to Eight Weeks to Mastery — focused entirely on the shots inside 100 yards that make up 60% of your score. The same proven week-by-week system, built specifically for the scoring zone.

⛳

Technique

Chips, pitches, bunker shots, and full wedges — built progressively from simple to complex, week by week.

🧠

Feel & Touch

Distance control, trajectory awareness, and the soft hands that separate good short games from great ones.

📋

Scoring Strategy

Shot selection, lie assessment, and on-course decision-making so you always pick the right play around the green.

Your 8-Week Journey

Each week builds on the last — from foundational technique through course-ready confidence.

Week 1

Understanding Your Tools

Wedge anatomy, bounce & grind, loft gapping, and building the foundation for every short game shot.

Week 2

The Chip Shot System

Setup fundamentals, the bump-and-run, club selection around the green, and building a reliable chipping motion.

Week 3

Pitching with Precision

The pitch vs. chip distinction, wrist hinge, trajectory control, and dialling in your landing spots.

Week 4

The Full Wedge Game

The clock system for distance control, partial wedge shots, and building your personal yardage chart.

Week 5

Bunker Confidence

Sand setup, the explosion shot, how bounce works in bunkers, and the mental shift that makes sand play easy.

Week 6

Specialty Shots

Flop shots, tight lies, downhill chips, rough escapes — the creative shots that save strokes.

Week 7

The Mental Short Game

Pre-shot routines, visualization, managing pressure, and building the mindset for clutch up-and-downs.

Week 8

Integration & Course Play

Putting it all together — shot selection, lie assessment, course strategy, and building your scoring-zone game plan.
