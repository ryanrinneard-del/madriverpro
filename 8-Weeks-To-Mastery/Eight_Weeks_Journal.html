How to Use This Journal

One Book. Eight Weeks. Three Sessions.

This journal mirrors the book chapter for chapter. Each week has three short sessions — before you play, during your round, and after you're done. There's also a reusable practice page for range work.

You don't need to fill out everything every time. The goal is honest, consistent awareness — not more pressure. Even one note per round makes a difference.

Week One · Chapter 1

The Two Games

This week you're learning to notice the difference between thinking and trusting.

"The mind messes up more shots than the body."

— Tommy Bolt
🌅

Before You Play

Complete this before your round or practice — takes 3 minutes

This Week's Focus

Notice which game you're playing. When you hit a shot, ask: was that the outer game (mechanics, control) or the inner game (feel, trust)? You don't need to fix anything yet. Just notice.

Week Two · Chapter 2

Awareness Over Anxiety

This week you're learning to observe tension instead of fighting it.

"The first step toward change is awareness. The second is acceptance."

— Nathaniel Branden
🌅

Before You Play

3 minutes before you start

This Week's Focus

Anxiety isn't the enemy — resistance is. This week, when nerves show up, don't fight them. Just name what you feel. "I'm tight in my shoulders." "My grip is getting stronger." That's all. Observe, don't fix.
