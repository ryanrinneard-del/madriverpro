Coaching

Groups & Clinics

There's something powerful about improving alongside others. Group sessions and clinics create the kind of energy and encouragement that makes learning feel natural — and a lot more fun. Bring friends, meet new ones, and watch your game grow together.

Find your group.

Group Clinics

For Mad River members and their guests. Focused group sessions covering full swing, short game, putting, or course play.

Short Game School

A half-day deep dive into the scoring shots — chipping, pitching, bunker play, and putting. Limited to 4 spots per session.

Non-Member Group Clinics

No membership required. Group sessions open to everyone looking to improve their game in a supportive setting.

Corporate & Private Groups

Team-building events, group outings, and private clinics tailored to any group size and skill level.
