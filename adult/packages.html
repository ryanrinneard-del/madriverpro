What Happens In A Lesson

Five steps. Same arc, every time.

01

The 5-Minute Diagnosis

We pull up your portal. Recent rounds, stat trends, what came up in your last reflection. The lesson plans itself.

02

The TrackMan Block

Twenty to thirty minutes on the range, every shot tracked. Path, face, strike, launch, spin, carry. The numbers don't lie about what's happening at impact.

03

The Fix

One thing. Maybe two. We build it on the range until you can repeat it without thinking, then layer it under live conditions.

04

Pressure Test

A constraint. A target. A game with score on the line. Can you do it when it counts — that's the only question that matters.

05

The Walk-Off

One sentence about what to work on before next time, logged in your portal. You leave knowing exactly what to do, and so do I.
