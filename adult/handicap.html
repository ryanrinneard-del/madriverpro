How this is calculated

This is a portal index — a personal handicap tracker built from the rounds you've logged here. It mirrors the Golf Canada / World Handicap System formula closely but is not an official index. For that, post the same rounds on Golf Canada.

  • Per-round differential = (113 ÷ slope) × (score − rating)
  • Your index = average of your best N differentials from the last 20 rounds, where N depends on how many rounds you've posted (3 rounds: best 1 minus 2.0 · 6 rounds: best 2 minus 1.0 · 20 rounds: best 8)
  • Rounds need course rating + slope to count. Mad River scorecard rounds capture this automatically; other rounds need you to enter rating + slope on the totals form
  • Simplifications vs. official WHS: no Playing Conditions Calculation (PCC), no net-double-bogey adjustments. Close enough for self-tracking; off by a stroke or two at most
