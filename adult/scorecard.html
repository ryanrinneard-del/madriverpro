What does each column mean? (tap)
  • Hdcp — how hard the hole is, ranked 1 (hardest) to 18 (easiest)
  • Score — how many strokes you took on the hole
  • Tee shot — where your tee shot finished: Fairway, Left, Right, or OB / Penalty
  • Approach distance — how far away you were when you hit your shot into the green (in yards)
  • First putt (ft) — how far away your first putt was, in feet
  • Putts — how many putts you took on the green
  • Chip difficulty — if you missed the green: Easy (open chip) or Tough (short-sided / awkward lie)
  • Committed? — were you fully committed to that shot, or wavering?
  • 9 iron or less — tap if you had a 9-iron or shorter club for this approach (these are your scoring chances)
  • Penalty — number of penalty strokes on this hole
