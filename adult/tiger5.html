THE 5 MISTAKES THAT COST YOU SHOTS

Most golfers don't lose shots because of bad swings. They lose them from the same small mistakes, over and over again. Track these five, and you'll quickly see where your scores are really coming from.

  1. Bogey or worse on a Par 5. These are the easiest holes. You should be scoring here.
  2. Double bogey or worse. This is the big one. These destroy your score.
  3. Missed easy shot around the green. A simple chip or pitch that should have been close — but wasn't.
  4. Three-putt. Too many putts on the green.
  5. Bogey with a short club (9-iron or less). You were close enough to do better.
WHY THIS MATTERS

One round doesn't mean much. But:

  • 6 rounds → patterns start to show
  • 12 rounds → you know exactly what's hurting you

This is how you actually improve: fix the mistakes that happen all the time — not your swing every week.

HOW TO TRACK (KEEP IT SIMPLE)

Option 1 — Use your phone.

When one of these mistakes happens, tap it. That's it. It saves automatically, so you can close the tab, play your next shot, and come back later. After your round, tap Log this round → and everything saves for you.

Option 2 — Use paper.

Print the small card below. Put a checkmark when a mistake happens. After your round, open Submit Round and enter the totals.
