Five avoidable errors. Track live, hole by hole. Tiger's benchmark was ≤ 6 across a four-round tournament — he figured he'd win. For you: 1–2 or fewer per round is the working target.

HOW THIS WORKS

Two ways to use Tiger 5. Pick what fits your round.

Option A — Use your phone. Tap the counter below as each error happens. Your tally auto-saves, so you can close this tab, walk off to the next tee, open it again, and it's all still there. When the round's done, tap Log this round → and your counts flow straight into the scorecard for Ryan.

Option B — Carry paper. Print the card below (6 × 4", fits a standard scorecard holder). Tick the hole boxes as things happen. After the round, open Submit Round and type in your totals.
