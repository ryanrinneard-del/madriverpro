UPLOAD SWING VIDEO

Send Ryan a Clip

Upload a video for Ryan to review between sessions. Best results: face-on or down-the-line, full swing visible, 10–30 seconds, landscape orientation. Ryan gets an email with the upload and reviews in the portal or pulls to OnForm on his iPad.

Prototype note. Video files are saved to your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) and persist across reloads, but only on this device/browser. The production build wires this to Vercel Blob so uploads sync to the cloud (see README). Large files (> ~500 MB) may hit browser storage limits.
